Compliance foundation

Global growth needs privacy clarity before paid subscriptions.

The web beta should be explicit about microphone, camera, location, local storage, cookies, ads, and legal boundaries before scaling Google SEO, YouTube, TikTok, Reddit, or Facebook traffic.

Privacy Policy

Explain local microphone analysis, optional recording, camera use, GPS/place lookup, localStorage, IndexedDB, exports, and deletion.

Terms of Service

State that SOUNDTEST.PRO is for field documentation and complaint organization, not certified metrology or legal advice.

Cookie / ads consent

Before AdSense or analytics, add cookie disclosure and consent behavior suitable for GDPR-style markets.

Recording consent

Warn users that audio/video may capture voices, people, private spaces, and precise locations; local laws vary.

Data deletion

For local-only beta, users can delete local records. Cloud versions must add account deletion and cloud data deletion.

App store future

If native apps come later, add review notes, platform billing, privacy labels, native save flows, and background recording validation.

A simple global rule: collect less, explain more, and never market browser readings as official legal measurement.
