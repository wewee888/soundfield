Use case · neighbor noise recording

Build a clear noise diary before talking to neighbors, landlords, or property managers.

SOUNDTEST.PRO helps tenants and residents capture repeated noise events with dB trends, timestamps, evidence photos, optional recordings, GPS or room notes, and exportable reports.

Start documenting noiseCheck privacy model

What to record

Capture the time window, room, window state, repeated impact sounds, music, pets, HVAC, or night disturbance patterns.

What SOUNDTEST.PRO adds

dBA/dBC/dBZ, LAeq, L10/L90, manual event marks, evidence photos, optional audio/video, PDF, CSV, and manifest hashes.

How to share

Export a concise PDF summary first. Keep precise coordinates private unless you intentionally include them.

FAQ

  • Use multiple short sessions instead of one vague complaint.
  • Record from the same room and note doors/windows.
  • Save media files promptly from the Records page.
SOUNDTEST.PRO is not a certified sound level meter. For legal, regulatory, or formal dispute proof, verify with calibrated professional equipment.
