What you record

Same engine, six templates

  • Real-time dBA / dBC / dBZ with Fast / Slow time response.
  • LAeq, L10, L90 statistics and alert thresholds.
  • Evidence photos with live dB, GPS, place, calibration context.
  • Optional audio, video, manual event marks, and notes.
  • PDF, CSV, and JSON manifest exports — everything stays local unless you choose to share.
SOUNDTEST.PRO is not a certified sound level meter. For legal, regulatory, or formal dispute proof, verify with calibrated professional equipment and follow local procedures.
